Welcome to Our Clinic

Feel the warmth and energy the moment you walk through our doors. At Kids N Motion, our clinic is thoughtfully designed to spark joy, build confidence, and support every child’s sensory and emotional needs. With multiple sensory gyms featuring foam pits, crash pads, a rock wall, indoor playground structures, and a zip line, movement and play are at the heart of everything we do. We also offer cozy, calming spaces and quiet hideaways, creating the perfect balance of adventure and regulation.

All of our therapy rooms and gyms are equipped with one-way mirrors, allowing parents the opportunity to observe sessions discreetly. This encourages ongoing learning and collaboration, so parents can better understand their child’s therapy process and continue supporting progress at home. Every corner of our space is built to encourage exploration, connection, and growth in a fun, safe, and welcoming environment.
