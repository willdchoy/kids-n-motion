You Get My Child

Some moments are simply harder than they should be.

Transitions fall apart. Play doesn’t last. Communication feels frustrating. Small problems become big reactions. For some children the skills are still developing, and for others the skills don’t carry into real situations.

At Kids N Motion, children work individually with their therapist while learning alongside peers, so regulation, communication, and flexibility grow in the moments they’re actually needed.

What Makes Us Different

Most therapy teaches skills.
We help children use them.

Our sessions are intentionally scheduled so children are working individually with their therapist while other same-aged peers are also engaged in therapy. This creates natural opportunities to form connections, practice waiting, problem-solving, communication, and emotional regulation in a supportive environment

Movement, interaction, and guidance happen together, so progress becomes functional — not scripted.
